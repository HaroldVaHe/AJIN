# How to Incorporate a SAS in Colombia in 2025

The Simplified Stock Corporation (SAS) is the most popular corporate type in Colombia due to its flexibility and ease of incorporation.

## Advantages of the SAS

- **Limited liability**: Shareholders are liable only up to the amount of their contributions
- **Minimum shareholders**: Can be incorporated with just 1 person
- **No term limit**: Unlike other corporate types
- **Administrative flexibility**: Does not require a statutory auditor in all cases

## Steps to Incorporate a SAS

### 1. Draft the incorporation deed

It must include:
- Name, ID, and address of shareholders
- Company name followed by "S.A.S."
- Principal place of business
- Term of duration
- Corporate purpose

### 2. Authenticate the document

Before a notary public or through a private document.

### 3. Register with the Chamber of Commerce

- RUES form
- Commercial registration form
- Payment of registration tax

### 4. Obtain NIT from DIAN

- Single Tax Registry (RUT)
- Electronic invoicing

## Approximate Costs 2025

- **Legal fees**: $500,000 - $1,500,000 COP
- **Commercial registration**: $100,000 - $500,000 COP
- **Public deed**: $150,000 - $300,000 COP

**Total**: $750,000 - $2,300,000 COP
