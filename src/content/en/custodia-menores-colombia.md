# Everything You Need to Know About Child Custody in Colombia

Child custody is one of the most sensitive issues in family law. Know your rights and options.

## Types of Custody

### Sole or single-parent custody
One parent has custody of the child. The other has visitation and child support rights.

### Joint custody
Both parents share responsibility for the child's care in similar time periods.

### Third-party custody
Granted to relatives (grandparents, aunts/uncles) when neither parent can exercise it.

## Factors the Judge Evaluates

- Economic and emotional capacity of each parent
- Emotional bond with the child
- Stability of the family environment
- Child's opinion (if over 12 years old)
- History of domestic violence

## Legal Process

The process can be handled through:
- **Agreement between the parties**: At a notary or conciliation center
- **Judicial process**: When there is no agreement
